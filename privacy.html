中文版

我们非常重视用户的隐私和数据安全。以下是本应用所涉及的权限及其用途说明：

1. 读取电话状态 (READ_PHONE_STATE)

此权限用于识别设备信息，帮助我们进行用户识别、反作弊或统计分析等功能。我们不会收集、存储或上传任何通话内容或个人联系人信息。

2. 写入外部存储 (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

此权限用于保存游戏数据、图片缓存或用户设置等信息，以优化游戏体验。我们不会访问或修改用户存储中的个人文件（如照片、视频或文档）。

数据使用声明

  • 我们不会收集您的任何个人身份信息。
  • 我们不会将任何信息上传至服务器或与第三方共享。
  • 所有数据仅用于本地功能实现。

English Version

We value your privacy and data security. Below is an explanation of the permissions our app requests and how they are used:

1. READ_PHONE_STATE

This permission is used to identify the device for purposes such as anti-cheating and user analytics. We do not collect or store any call data or personal contact information.

2. WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

This permission is used to save game data, image cache, or user settings to improve the game experience. We do not access or modify any of your personal files (such as photos, videos, or documents).

Data Usage

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not upload any data to a server or share it with third parties.
  • All data is used locally for feature implementation.
